SHATTERING ILLUSION BIOPHILIA

Past exhibition
17 February - 1 April 2024
  • Overview
  • Works
  • Installation Views
Overview
 
‘Biophilia: Shattering Illusion’ stands to not only challenge our environmental awareness but also to revel in our profound connection to nature. The showcase spotlights a diverse array of artists, all united by a shared mission: the preservation of our planet through imaginative and creative pursuits.

Within this exhibition, artists employ a spectrum of approaches to contribute to environmental conservation. From utilizing sustainable materials to engaging in collaborative ventures with local communities for the repurposing of non-organic waste and even instigating impactful movements in far-flung regions such as Tibet, each artist brings a unique perspective to the global conversation on ecological consciousness. What sets the participants of this exhibition apart is their unwavering dedication to infusing eco-consciousness into their artistic practices.
'Biophilia: Shattering Illusion' not only serves as a visual feast but also as a powerful call to action, urging viewers to reflect on their own connection to nature and the role creativity can play in shaping a sustainable future.

Participating artists: Arahmaiani, Ari Bayuaji, Arin Dwihartanto, Attina Nuraini + Evan Driyananda, Elissa Gjertson (Plastik Kembali), Eun Vivian Lee, Franziska Fennert, Hedwige Jacobs, Ines Katamso, Mulyana, Nor Tijan Firdaus, and Wishulada Panthanuvong.
